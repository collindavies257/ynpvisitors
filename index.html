Yellowstone National Park Background

Yellowstone National Park is the largest national park in the United States. It is also the first National Park.

Its features comes from being on a supervolcano. These features attract people from all over the world.

Data Breakdown

The average number of vistors skyroceted after 2013. The sharp decline in 2020 was due to the COVID-19 pandemic which caused the gates to closed for over a month. That window is usually a very busy time of year for the park.

In 2021, the amount of park visitors peaked at 4.8 million people. That's a 1 million person jump compared to 2020. This was due to a backlog of people who couldn't vacation in 2020. People being able to work virtually and on the road also had an impact.
